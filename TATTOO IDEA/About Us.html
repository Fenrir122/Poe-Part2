About Cresent Tattoo Inks

We have began this studio as way of reaching individuals around our Communinity, we are an out-reach program assisting local artists
as of now we are only a start-up company.
We take Pride in helping our Communinities and we will continue to do so in the future. Cresent Moon Inks is for individuals whom are seeking to better themself through art and find unique ways to express themselves in good and productive ways that will help better themselves and their communities.

Our vision

Our bussines vision for the future is deeply rooted in helping our poor and struggling communities around Port Elizabeth. Plans are to improve as much of the community trough charity work and with our small bussines providing an enviroment for our youth to learn new ways of making it out of the townships giving them a chance to go out in the world an make their own mark.

History

Our bussines started at 2010.Since our start-up in 2010 we have established ourselves in the community by assisting up-and-coming artists through paid.

Since our inception at 2010 we have helped about 541-young individuals that ended up opening their own shops and some of those bussines went to be successful.

Goals

By the end of 2025-2026 we estimate the growth of up to 200 establishments across South Africa in all provinces. Our ambitious goals are supported by the fast growth we've had in the past 5 years. With continued progress Cresent Moon Inks will continue to help out the community, by helping the youth our future to progress thus ensuring a brighter tomorrow with individuals that will inturn help progress South Africa little by little
